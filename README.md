# BANK-LOAN-DATA-ANALYSIS
INTRODUCTION

The banking sector relies on effective loan management to ensure profitability and reduce risk. Analyzing loan data through traditional methods can be time-consuming and less insightful. 

Therefore, this project uses a dashboard to visually analyze key aspects such as loan applications, funded amounts, repayments, borrower income, and risk levels. Identify trends, evaluate loan performance, and understand borrower behavior to support better decision-making and improve overall loan management.

By analyzing these factors, the bank can make better decisions regarding loan approvals, risk management, and business growth strategies.
